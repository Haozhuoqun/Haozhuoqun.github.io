Kiev-4 is a copy of Zeiss Ikon Contax III rangefinder, made by the Soviet Union.

My kiev-4 has a jupiter-8 (ЮПИТЕР-8) lens with Arsenal factory logo instead of KMZ one. The lens has a beautiful coating.
